ABOUT

The Motive

In today’s world, screens are everywhere—our workspaces, our homes, and even our pockets. While technology has brought us closer and made life easier, it has also quietly taken over more of our time than we realize. At ScreenWise, we believe in creating a harmonious balance between technology and real-life moments. Our mission is to empower you to take back control of your time and focus on what truly matters.

The Problem

Did you know that the average person spends over 7 hours a day on screens? Excessive screen time has been linked to a range of issues, including decreased productivity, eye strain, poor sleep quality, and even mental health challenges like anxiety and depression. Many of us feel trapped in an endless cycle of notifications, social media scrolling, and binge-watching, but breaking free seems daunting.

The Solution

That’s where ScreenWise comes in. Our app is designed to help you:

  • Track your screen usage habits to better understand where your time goes.
  • Set Reminders that encourage healthy screen breaks and offline activities.
  • Stay Focused with tools to block distracting apps and notifications.
  • Transform Your Habits with gamified challenges and rewards for meeting your screen time goals.

TIPS

DO's

Set Daily Screen Time Goals

Track and limit your screen usage to specific hours each day to stay mindful of your habits.

Take Regular Breaks

Follow the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.

Engage in Offline Activities

Replace screen time with hobbies like reading, exercising, or spending quality time with family and friends.

DON'Ts

Avoid Screens Before Bedtime

Refrain from using screens 1-2 hours before sleeping, as blue light can disrupt your sleep cycle.

Don’t Use Screens During Meals

Keep mealtimes screen-free to focus on mindful eating and engaging with those around you.

Don’t Ignore Notifications

Manage your app notifications to avoid unnecessary distractions and interruptions.
